#  North

**Build better habits, one day at a time.**

HabitFlow is a powerful, all-in-one React Native application designed to help you build consistency, track your daily progress, and journal your journey. Whether you want to read more, workout daily, or drink more water, HabitFlow keeps you accountable with visual streaks and detailed insights.

##  Key Features

1. Smart Habit Management: Easily add, edit, and customize habits with specific time slots and categories.
2. Streak Tracking visualize your consistency with a dynamic weekly calendar and streak counters. Miss a day? The app helps you get back on track.
3. Instant Progress Updates:Optimistic UI updates ensure your check-ins feel instant and snappy.
4. notebook Journaling: Keep notes for every habit. Reflect on your wins or write down why you missed a task directly within the app.
5. Visual Analytics: View your completion rates through interactive circular progress bars and color-coded heatmaps.
